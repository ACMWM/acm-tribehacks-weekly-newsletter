Steer Clear is looking for developers

Steer Clear is looking developers! They are about to release an app to replace their calling system. Now you will be able to request rides directly from your iPhone or Android device! They are looking for an iOS, Android, and backend developer. The backend is python/flask and easy to follow. Steer Clear has partnered with our Computer Science department to offer student developers 1 credit per semester for maintaining the Steer Clear app. For those new to Steer Clear, the service is a safe-ride program at WM that provides a reliable, secure and free means of travel for students on weekend nights when school is in session. Contact Uli for more information.
