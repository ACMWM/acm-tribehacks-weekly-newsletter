Do Something Epic!

Information Session: November 30th, 7pm

Epic is a national leader in software development for healthcare systems. They create and implement software for many of the largest healthcare organizations in the US; around 54% of Americans are being cared for by clinicians who use Epic software. Epic is also expanding internationally. A William & Mary alumn will discuss opportunities and life at this fast-growing organization. Dinner will be provided.
