Amazon Coming to Campus

Amazon is coming to campus next week and they're hosting two events:

Amazon Open House: Wednesday, October 28th, 2-4PM
Description: Amazon recruiter and '99 William & Mary alum, Kevin Pryor, will be available for 1-1 or group conversations.
Location: Cohen Career Center, Atrium

Amazon Information Session: Wednesday, October 28th, 5-7PM
Description: Amazon recruiter and '99 William & Mary alum, Kevin Pryor, will highlight internship and full-time opportunities, and provide advice on the application process and working at Amazon.
Location: Cohen Career Center Register here.
